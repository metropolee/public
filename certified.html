What is a Metropolee certification?

Fantastic apps are always based on a winning idea. If we can fulfil a need, people will download our app. We created Metropolee observing two of the biggest needs people have nowadays. People need an app, and some people need someone to set-up their app. It really is that simple.

We provide the app, we provide the training for you to become an app consultant, and we provide the network. Just put in the will to be a part of all this, and we take care of the rest.

How does it work

Our certification program consists of a series of 90-second videos that detail step-by-step processes, followed by simple tests in written, multiple choice. Once you’ve met Metropolee’s standards, you will be awarded with our certificate and enjoy the benefits of being part of a big team.
